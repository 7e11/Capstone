When your hands are simply not enough

Jestr is web based application that bridges the gap between keyboard and mouse to a more natural freedom of expression. Jestr allows uses to utilize hand gestures in front of their camera to boost thier productivity via key bindings the user defined. Have you ever wanted to lower the volume of your epic music but hands are full? Need to pause a video but too far from the keyboard? Or is Covid too real for you?

We also offer voice assisted operations for an even more seamless experience.
